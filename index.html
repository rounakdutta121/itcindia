Our Core Testing Capabilities

Medical Device Testing

Precision compliance for life-saving technology, ensuring patient safety, biocompatibility, and reliability as per global frameworks.

  • • Electrical safety & performance
  • • Biocompatibility evaluation
  • • Risk management verification

EMC / EMI Testing

Advanced compatibility testing ensuring devices operate reliably without causing or experiencing interference.

  • • Radiated & Conducted emissions
  • • ESD & Surge protection
  • • RF immunity testing

IP Testing

Certified protection testing against solid particles and water (IP65 to IP69K) for demanding environments.

  • • Dust Tight (IP6X)
  • • High-pressure washdown (IP69K)
  • • Immersion testing (IP67/68)

JSS 55555 Testing

Military-grade environmental testing for defense and aerospace equipment under extreme operational conditions.

  • • High/Low temp endurance
  • • Vibration & Mechanical shock
  • • Salt fog & Corrosion

Electrical Safety

Verification of product safety to protect users from hazards across residential and industrial environments.

  • • Dielectric strength (Hi-Pot)
  • • Leakage current measurement
  • • Earth continuity testing

Luminaire Testing

Comprehensive photometric and safety analysis for indoor, outdoor, commercial, and industrial lighting systems.

  • • Lumen output and efficacy
  • • Color temperature and rendering accuracy
  • • Thermal endurance
